[EU] EMA Approves mCombriax: First Combined mRNA Vaccine for COVID-19 and Flu

The EMA's Committee for Medicinal Products for Human Use (CHMP) recommended mCombriax for approval in February 2026. This represents the first combined messenger RNA vaccine for COVID-19 and seasonal influenza, targeting adults aged 50 and older—a significant breakthrough in immunization marketing and patient education strategies.

[Global] FDA Approves Eli Lilly's Foundayo (Orforglipron) for Weight Management

The FDA approved Foundayo on April 1, 2026, an oral GLP-1 receptor agonist for treating obesity and weight-related conditions. This oral formulation represents a significant competitive development in the crowded GLP-1 market, creating new marketing opportunities and competitive pressures in the weight management space.

→ FDA

[Global] FDA Approves Generic Dapagliflozin: 14 Companies Approved for Production

On April 7, 2026, the FDA approved 14 generic manufacturers of dapagliflozin (5 mg and 10 mg tablets), including Teva, Sandoz, and Indian manufacturers like Aurobindo, Biocon, and Cipla. This decision significantly expands market competition for this established SGLT2 inhibitor, signaling continued generic competition in the diabetes therapeutic area.

[Global] FDA Seeks Legislative Authority Over Misleading DTC Advertising

The FDA has requested new powers to regulate misleading direct-to-consumer (DTC) advertising, citing "deceptive and frequently confusing" claims in digital and social media. This development signals stricter compliance requirements and potential restrictions on promotional messaging across channels.
