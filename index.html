📈 Trends to Watch

Digital-First Patient Engagement

With DTP programs expanding and digital ad spend dwarfing traditional media, pharma marketing is entering an era where digital channels are the primary — not supplementary — patient touchpoint. European markets should expect similar shifts as regulatory frameworks adapt.

AI in Marketing Compliance

Both the FDA (with AI-powered ad surveillance) and pharma marketers (optimizing content for AI-driven HCP tools) are racing to harness artificial intelligence. Expect regulatory and promotional AI strategies to co-evolve rapidly throughout 2026.

European Regulatory Modernization

From ePI pilots to new variation guidelines to revised pharmaceutical legislation, Europe's regulatory environment is undergoing its biggest transformation in decades. Marketing and medical affairs teams must adapt communications strategies quickly to stay compliant and competitive.
